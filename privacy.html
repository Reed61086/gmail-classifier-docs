Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

Information We Collect

The Gmail Email Classifier extension requires access to your Gmail account to:

  • Read email content for classification purposes
  • Create and apply labels to your emails
  • Modify email organization through labels

How We Use Your Information

All email processing is done locally on your device. We do not store or transmit your email content to any external servers. The extension only uses the Gmail API to:

  • Read emails for local classification
  • Apply labels based on classifications
  • Manage email organization

Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. The extension:

  • Processes all data locally on your device
  • Does not store email content
  • Uses secure OAuth 2.0 for Gmail API access
  • Never shares your data with third parties
